Privacy Policy & Services

Welcome to LivFit.pro. We respect your privacy and are committed to keeping things simple, secure, and transparent.

1. No User Accounts

No sign-ups, no logins. Everything is freely available.

2. No Cookies

We don’t track your activity. No cookies, no worries.

3. Donations Are Optional

You can support us with donations, but it's 100% optional.

4. No Personal Data Collected

We don’t ask for your name, email, or payment info. Payment gateways handle donations securely.

5. No Third-Party Trackers

We do not use analytics, ads, or external trackers.

6. Contact Us

Questions? Email us at contact@livfit.pro

Our Services

LivFit.pro is your all-in-one free fitness & wellness hub. Explore what we offer below:

Zim Workouts

Personalized muscle gain and fat loss routines. Whether you're a beginner or advanced, access structured plans to build strength and achieve your fitness goals with proper gym-based training.

Yoga

Promote mental clarity and flexibility with step-by-step yoga flows. Ideal for stress relief, joint mobility, and inner peace. No equipment required — just a mat and your breath.

Calisthenics

Master your body weight through progressive training routines. Build strength and agility with zero equipment. Perfect for home workouts and street fitness lovers.

Meditation

Improve focus, reduce anxiety, and feel calm with guided meditation audios and videos. Choose from short mindful sessions to longer deep relaxation practices.

Diet Plans

Structured meal plans for weight loss, muscle gain, or balanced living. Backed by nutrition science and easy to follow, with vegetarian, vegan, and high-protein options.

Other Tools

Access BMI calculators, macro trackers, fitness articles, lifestyle tips, and much more — all curated to keep your health journey smart and simple.
